Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: July 7, 2026

The Vibe Upgrade participates in affiliate marketing programs. In plain terms: some of the links we publish — on this website and on our Pinterest boards at @thevibeupgrade — are affiliate links. If you click one and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission. This never changes the price you pay.

Programs we participate in

  • Amazon Associates — as an Amazon Associate, we earn from qualifying purchases.
  • Impact-network retailers (such as The Home Depot) — retail affiliate programs managed through the Impact partnership platform.

How we choose products

We only feature products that fit our niche — analog living spaces and everyday wellness — and that we would genuinely put in our own rooms. Commissions do not determine what we feature; fit does. We write our own descriptions and do not accept payment from brands for placement.

FTC compliance

This disclosure is provided in accordance with the U.S. Federal Trade Commission's guidelines on endorsements and testimonials (16 CFR Part 255). Pins containing affiliate links are labeled in accordance with the platform's disclosure requirements.

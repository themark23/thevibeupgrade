What is The Vibe Upgrade?

The Vibe Upgrade is a curation project about making rooms feel human again. We gather ideas, inspiration, and carefully chosen products for two things we care about: analog living spaces — vinyl corners, typewriter desks, film-camera shelves, reading nooks lit by warm Edison bulbs — and everyday wellness rituals, from slow mornings and herbal tea to bath rituals and better sleep.

You'll find our curation primarily on Pinterest at @thevibeupgrade, organized into boards by room and ritual.

🎵 The Analog Room

Turntables and vinyl storage, typewriters, film cameras, board-game tables, and the warm lighting that ties it together.

📚 The Reading Nook

Chairs worth sinking into, shelves worth filling, and lamps that make paper pages glow.

🌿 Everyday Wellness

Morning rituals, movement at home, bath and wind-down routines, herbal tea, and genuinely better sleep.

🕯️ Slow Living

Fewer screens, softer light, more texture. Small upgrades that change how a room feels.

How we publish

We research products and ideas in our niche, write our own descriptions, and share them as pins on our own Pinterest boards on a regular schedule.
